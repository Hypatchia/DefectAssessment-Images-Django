Overview

A pump impeller is a component that rotates within a submersible pump to move fluids.
The design varies for different applications and fluid types.
Below is an Image of the Impeller that this tool can assess with 98% accuracy:

Scope

In a production line, the impeller is placed on a conveyor belt and a camera takes a picture of the impeller.
The image is then analyzed by a machine learning model to determine whether the impeller has a front defect or not.
If the impeller has a front defect, it is removed from the conveyor belt.
If the impeller does not have a front defect, it is placed in a box and shipped to customers.

Explore more about submersible pump impellers on Google.
